Set in Stone

Stick Figure

Released November 13, 2015

Stick Figure's third studio album, this melodic reggae song set includes appearances by Slightly Stoopid and Rebelution among others. With all songs written by Massachusetts native and musican/producer Scott Woodruff, this album put Stick Figure at the top of the radar for any American reggae fans. This album was three years in the making and includings 14 brand new tracks.

Band Members

Scott Woodruff - Writer, Vocals, Guitar
Tommy Sullivan - Bass
Kevin Offitzer - Drums
KBong - Keyboards and Vocals
