Specializing in gourmet versions of fast food restaurant menu items, Restaurant Renaud is proud to call the seacoast home for over 40 years. With a focus on locally sourced ingredients and an in-house brewery, Restaurant Renaud offers the comfort of a small family restaurant with the offerings of an upscale five-star establishment.
